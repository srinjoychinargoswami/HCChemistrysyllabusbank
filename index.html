City University of New York: Hunter College Chemistry Course Syllabi Directory

This website has all the syllabi for all the Chemistry Courses (Core Major, Elective and Non-Major) offered at Hunter College and the Collateral Courses required to earn a degree in Chemistry from Hunter College.

Degree maps will be available. While all the requirements are not complete, they hopefully will be soon

Textbooks can be accessed by clicking the Access Textbook PDFs and following the folders.

All the syllabi posted here are all crowdsourced so thank you to all who have contributed!
